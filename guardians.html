We created the Aztec Gold Rum foundation: ‘Guardians of the
Rainforest’ because we believe passionately that we can make a
difference to help protect the rainforests in Panama from modern-day
harm and deforestation.

We help preserve the natural beauty of the landscape in Panama and
promote the adventure, life and joy they bring us. Discover the
undiscovered, bring hidden landscapes back to life and create flavours
harmonious with the depths of the Panamanian jungle.

Aztec Rum leads a forward-thinking world of spirits that challenge the
use of our resources and the preservation of our landscape.

We have partnered exclusively with Panama Wildlife Conservation
(PWCC) to make significant donations that help fund voluntary
expeditions and work in the field.
